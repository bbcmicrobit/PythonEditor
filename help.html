Start Here

This is a very high level overview of Python, MicroPython, the micro:bit and the general modus operandi.

Some example Python code:

import microbit

fortunes = [
    "Never step off a moving bus",
    "This sentence is false",
    "The meaning of life is overrated",
    "Do not touch!",
    "You will receive some advice",
    "My hovercraft is full of eels",
    ]

while True:
    if microbit.button_a.is_pressed():
         i = microbit.random(len(fortunes))
         cookie = fortunes[i]
         microbit.display.scroll_string(cookie)
